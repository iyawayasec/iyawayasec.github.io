IYAWAYA SECONDARY SCHOOL SHORT HISTORY

Iyawaya secondary school is a core day school with a total of 20 hectares located at Inyala ward, Mbeya rural district in Mbeya region,Tanzania.The school was founded on 10th of february, 2022 having 5 teachers (four males and one female ) after reduction of students at Imezu secondary school when the central government of Tanzania assigned 160 milions Tzs for the construction of 8 classrooms as the implementation of covid-19 projects.



The school was registered on 21st February 2022 with registration number S.5756 and centre number S.6469 and since then it was under headmistress TIBUS NAPHER MAKONA until 28th March 2022 when she was transferred to another school and SALMIN JUMANNE MWACHILUWI was appointed as an acting headmaster for both schools.



On 20th April 2023, Mr. SALMIN JUMANNE MWACHILUWI was promoted to headmaster at Ikholo secondary school and BILEI ANDERSON CHAGOHA from Malama secondary school became the headmaster of Iyawaya secondary school up to date.



Today the school has a total of 15 teachers (11 being males and 5 females) and 431 students from form one to form four.
